About Aqua Safari

Aqua Safari offers authentic Shikara boat tours through the serene backwaters of Alappuzha (Alleppey), Kerala. Our traditional Kerala Shikara boats provide the perfect vantage point to experience the unique beauty of Kerala's waterways in comfort and style.

Our guided tours take you through scenic canals and open backwaters, showcasing local village life, picturesque landscapes, and excellent photography spots. During the journey, we stop at traditional toddy shops where you can experience authentic Kerala cuisine and culture at your leisure.

What We Offer

Experience the Magic of Kerala Backwaters

Our carefully curated boat tours offer you an unforgettable journey through the picturesque backwaters of Kerala. Glide through tranquil waters, witness local life along the banks, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this unique ecosystem.

  • Journey through picturesque village canals with traditional bridges
