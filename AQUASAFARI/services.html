Shikara Boat Experience

Our traditional Shikara boats offer the most authentic way to explore Kerala's famous backwaters. These beautifully crafted wooden boats provide a peaceful and scenic journey through the palm-fringed canals.

  • Handcrafted from premium wood with colorful canopies
  • Comfortable seating with cushions and backrests
  • Experience authentic village life along the waterways
  • Birdwatching and photography tours available
  • Experienced boatmen who know the waterways intimately

Houseboat Experience

Our traditional Kerala houseboats (kettuvallams) offer the ultimate backwater experience. These floating villas provide comfortable overnight stays with modern amenities while maintaining authentic Kerala architecture.

  • Handcrafted from premium wood and coir
  • Spacious bedrooms with attached bathrooms
  • Open lounge area with panoramic views
  • Delicious Kerala cuisine prepared onboard
  • Experienced crew including chef and captain

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a Shikara boat ride?

You can book directly through our website, by calling our reservation number, or through WhatsApp. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially during peak season.

How do your taxi and accommodation services work?

We coordinate with trusted local partners for taxi and accommodation services. When you book through us, we'll connect you with the service provider, but payments are made directly to them. We ensure quality service through our partner network.

What is your cancellation policy for bad weather?

Your safety is our priority. In case of severe weather conditions (floods, cyclones, heavy storms), boat services will be canceled and advance payments will be fully refunded. We monitor weather conditions closely and will notify you in advance if any changes are needed.

Can you arrange airport pickup?

Yes! We can coordinate airport transfers through our trusted taxi partners. Please provide your flight details when booking, and we'll arrange your pickup.

Do you offer package deals?

Yes! We offer attractive packages that combine boat rides with taxi services and accommodation. Contact us with your travel plans and we'll create a customized itinerary for you.
