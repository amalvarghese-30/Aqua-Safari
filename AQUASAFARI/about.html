Our Story

Discover the passion behind Kerala's finest boating experiences

Welcome to Aqua Safari

Founded in 2005, Aqua Safari has grown from a single houseboat operation to Alleppey's premier provider of diverse backwater experiences. We now offer a range of services including traditional Shikara rides, luxurious houseboat stays, adventurous kayaking, and thrilling speedboat rides.

Our mission is to showcase the breathtaking beauty of Kerala's backwaters while preserving their natural splendor for future generations.

Our Expanded Services

While we specialize in backwater experiences, we're happy to arrange additional services through our trusted local partners:

  • Houseboats: Overnight stays with full amenities
  • Kayaking: Guided tours through narrow canals
  • Speedboats: Thrilling rides on open waters
  • Taxi Services: We can arrange airport transfers and local transportation through our partner taxi operators
  • Accommodation: We'll connect you with quality hotels and resorts near our location
  • Pickup Services: We can coordinate your pickup from airport, railway station or bus stand

Note: These services are provided by third-party operators. Payments for taxis and accommodation are made directly to the service providers.
